BEFORE ANESTHESIA

Anesthesia as not used in Europe or America until the mid-1800s. Before 1846, surgical procedures were done without the use of anesthesia. Patients stayed conscious as doctors attempted to finish the surgery as quickly as they could. Sometimes, methods such as opium or alcohol intoxication were used to lessen the pain of the patients. Other times, sedatives were used.

PRE-ANESTHESIA ISSUES

Unfortunately, even with such methods, subjects were never fully mollified during medical procedures and had to endure excruciating conditions when being operated on. Additionally, such treatments were also significantly unhealthy for subjects and may have worsened health conditions rather than improved such.
